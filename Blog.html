Paris, France

The City of Light is a timeless destination filled with romance, history, and world-class cuisine. Explore iconic lankmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and a charming cafes along the Seine.

Kyoto, Japan

Step into Japan's rich heritage in Kyoto - Home to ancient temples, traditional tea houses, and ironic cherry blossoms. Form the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove to Fushimi Inari Shrine, every corner is a cultural gem.

Santorini, Greece

Known for its whitewashed houses and stunning sunsets, Santorini is a dream destination. Stroll through charming cliffside villages, soak in volcanic hot springs, and enjoy fresh seafood by the Aegean Sea.

New-York City, USA

The city never sleeps offers something for everyone - skycrapers, Broadway shows, iconic food, and a cultural landmarks like Times Square, Centeral Park, and the Statue of Liberty.

Kerala, India

From backwaters to emerald tea plantations, Kerala is a tropical paradise in South India. Cruise through houseboat canals, Ayurvedic treatments and expllore the rich culture of traditional dance.
